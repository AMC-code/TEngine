(TEngine Controls)

(General Controls)
(r) sets respawn point
(.) respawn
(`) open/close menu 

(Player Controls)
(w) speed up
(s) speed down
(a) movement left
(d) movement right
(spaceBar) jump

(Vehicle Controls)
(w) movement up
(s) movement down
(a) movement left
(d) movement right
(c) cruise control
